Time series analysis using fable & modeltime

Case study: Mobility trends in Mexico in the COVID-19 pandemic

Pablo Benavides-Herrera

2020-09-18

The data

  • The dataset is provided by Google and consists of daily data regarding mobility in Mexico and each of its 32 states.

  • It provides time series showing the percentage change in visits to six different type of places, including:

    • Grocery & pharmacy
    • Parks
    • Transit stations
    • Retail & recreation
    • Residential
    • Workplaces

  • Changes for each day are compared to a baseline value, which is the median for the corresponding day of the week during the period of Jan 3- Feb 6.
